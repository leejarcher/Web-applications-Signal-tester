The current application, Signal tester, is being written primarily on an Android phone using the web audio API to act as a signal generator along with a peak reading level meter, basic oscilloscope and spectrum analyser. The generator and analyser use seperate browser windows that are navigated between using the buttons at the top of the two screens.

The generator provides the standard waveforms plus a custom and WAV waveform option. There are two generators that can be mixed together or waveform 2, at the bottom, can amplitude or frequency modulate the top waveform. Generator two can additionally take its waveform source from the microphone.

The output level of each oscillator is variable between -10 and +10, with negative values indicating a phase inversion of 180°.

The two custom waveforms use fourier coefficients to generate the waveforms, additive synthesis, with both files holding the coefficients being updateable. The WAV waveforms, again, can be modified by supplying your own WAV files.

WARNING: Please keep the volume low while using this app to avoid potential speaker/hearing damage.
